- Continued independence

**IPO Challenges**:
- Market conditions and timing
- Higher revenue threshold ($100M+)
- Public company compliance costs
- Quarterly earnings pressure

**Decision Criteria**:
- If $100M+ ARR achieved by Year 5 → Consider IPO
- If strategic offers exceed 15x ARR → Take acquisition
- If market conditions weak → Wait for strategic buyer
